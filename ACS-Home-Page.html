Home Screen

The Home screen gives you an overview of what’s happening with your orders currently. It is tailored according to your role. On Home screen, you can do multiple functions such as, Bulk Upload, create a New Order, Internal KIT Order, Change the status of Order, Cancel The Order, Download the Report, Filter and change the Fulfilment Centre (FC).

Change FC

You can change the Fulfilment Centre (FC) on the home screen. To change the FC, click on the FC Drop and Down button present on the Home screen Top Side as shown

List of FC will come as shown below:

Now, click on the applicable FC which you want, the selected FC detail will be visible on the screen.

Change Password

If you want to update your password, then click on the Profile Drop and Down button as shown.

Once you click on the Drop & Down button, the Forgot password option will come below the profile tab as shown below

Now, click on the change password, the change password window will open as shown below.

Now, enter you Existing Password in the Current Password Tab, New Password in New Password Tab and again enter your new password in the Confirm Password Tab.

Now, click on the Submit button, your password will be updated.
