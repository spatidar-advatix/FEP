Exception

An exception occurs when the there is some issue while delivering an order.

To go to Exception, go to Fulfilment > Exceptions

In exception module you can downloads the reports where exceptions occur in last 24 Hours in FC, Carrier, Process of the Exceptions, Address Check Failed and Exception Logs.

24hrs FC Exceptions

If for any order, there is no updates in last 24 hours then they will reflect in this list.

In this page you can see the list of exceptions orders list of last 24 hours.

To see the exceptions list of 24 hours FC order, go to Fulfilment > Exceptions > 24Hr FC Exceptions, the 24Hr FC Exceptions page will come on the screen a shown below.

On 24Hr FC Exceptions page, you can see the details of the exception’s orders i.e. Order Number, External Reference, Status, Tracking ID, Customer, Ship To, Ship To Address, Delivery Type, Carrier and Carrier Tracking, Line of Business, Reason, Company Name, Created On, Ship By Date and Estimated Delivery Date.

On 24Hr FC Exceptions page, you can download the report by clicking on the download button as shown above, or can be filter the 24Hr FC Exceptions page with the help of filter tab.

24hrs Carrier Exceptions

In this page you can see the list of carrier exceptions orders list of last 24 hours.

To see the exceptions list of 24 hours’ carrier order, go to Fulfilment > Exceptions > 24Hr Carrier Exceptions, the Carrier Exceptions page will come on the screen a shown below.

On 24Hr Carrier Exceptions page, you can see the details of the exception’s shipments i.e., Order Number, External Reference, Status, Tracking ID, Customer, Ship To, Ship To Address, Delivery Type, Carrier and Carrier Tracking ID, Line of Business, Reason, Company Name, Created On, Ship By Date and Estimated Delivery Date.

On Carrier Exceptions page, you can download the report by clicking on the download button as shown above, or can be filter the FC Exceptions page with the help of filter tab.

Address Check Failed

Sometimes if address is not correct or not identify the address by the system then they will reflect it in Address check failed.

Exception Logs

If there is some issue in some orders like address is wrong or inventory mismatched etc., those type of orders list reflect in the Exceptions Logs.

On Exceptions Log page, you can see the details of the exception’s orders i.e. Order Number, External Reference, Status, Tracking ID, Customer, Ship To, Ship To Address, Delivery Type, Carrier and Carrier Tracking ID, Line of Business, Reason, Company Name, Created On, Ship By Date and Estimated Delivery Date.

On Exceptions Log page, you can download the report by clicking on the download button as shown above, or can be filter the FC Exceptions page with the help of filter tab.
